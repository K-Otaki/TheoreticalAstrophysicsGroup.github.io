--- lang: en abstract: 'By use large cosmological Smoothed-Particle-Hydrodynamics simulations, we explore observed properties of Lyman alpha emitters (LAEs). We develop a phenomenological model of absorption, scattering and escape of Lyman alpha (Lyalpha) photons motivated by Neufeld (1991). In our model, we assume that the clumpiness of the inter-stellar medium in a galaxy is correlated with the larger scale substructure richness. As a result, we reproduce, for the first time, all of the following observed properties of LAEs at z=3.1: Lyalpha luminosity function, UV luminosity function of LAEs, the angular correlation function and the equivalent width distribution of Lya line.
