--- lang: ja abstract: Cosmological implications of clusters of galaxies are discussed with particular attention to their impor- tance in probing the cosmological parameters. More specifically we compute the number counts of clusters of galaxies, Log N - Log S relation, in X-ray and submm bands on the basis of the Press-Schechter theory. As an important step toward breaking the degeneracy among the viable cosmological models, we observed the most luminous X-ray cluster RXJ1347-1145 in three bands (21 and 43 GHz in the Nobeyama Radio Observatory, Japan, and 350 GHz in the J. C. Maxwell telescope at Mauna Kea, Hawaii).
