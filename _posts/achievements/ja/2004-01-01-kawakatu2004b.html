--- lang: ja abstract: Based on the radiation hydrodynamic model for the black hole(BH) growth and incorporating the chemical evolution of the early-type host galaxy, we construct the coevolution model of the quasar(QSO)BH and the host galaxy. As a result, it is found that after a galactic wind epoch, the luminosity is shifted from the host-dominant phase to the active galactic nucleus-dominant phase(QSO phase) in the timescale of a few 108yr. The former phase corresponds to the early stage of a growing BH and can be regarded as a ``proto-QSO'' phase.
