--- lang: en abstract: We study formation scenario of clusters of galaxies by calculation of statistical indicators from numerical simulation results (Yoshikawa et al.). Since formation epoch of clusters depends on formation scenario, open CDM or Lambda CDM, and young clusters have substructures, fraction of clusters with substructures are expected to be related with scenario. We obtain statistical indicators for X-ray surface brightness and total mass clumn density.
