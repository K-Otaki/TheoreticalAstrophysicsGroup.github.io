--- lang: en abstract: 'To explain the broad-band spectral energy distributions (SED) of Seyfert nuclei and QSOs, we study the emission spectrum emerging from a vertical disk-corona structure composed of a two-temperature plasma by solving hydrostatic equilibrium and radiative transfer self-consistently. Our model can nicely reproduce the soft X-ray excess with alpha ( Lnu ∝ nu- alpha) of about 1.5 and the hard tail extending to ˜50 keV with alpha˜0.5.
