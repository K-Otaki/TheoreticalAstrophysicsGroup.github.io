--- lang: en abstract: Rapid fluctuation with a frequency dependence of 1/falpha (with alpha =~ 1-2) is characteristic of radiation from black-hole objects. Its origin remains poorly understood. We examined three-dimensional magnetohydrodynamical simulation data, finding that a magnetized accretion disk exhibits both 1/falpha fluctuation (with alpha =~ 2) and a fractal magnetic structure (with the fractal dimension of D ~ 1.9).
